I am Matthew Weatherford. I am a full stack developer in Austin, TX. In my current job at WorkMonger, I am developing a system to match jobseekers with employers who are seeking non-teaching professionals in the education sector. I am using a variety of tools and languages, including isomorphic React, Redux, MySQL, and AWS. My expertise is in building single page apps with JavaScript frameworks. I also enjoy working with in the fields of the internet of things, machine learning, and virtual reality. I believe that apps should be thoroughly tested and designed with a customer first approach. During my undergraduate and graduate studies, I studied philosophy. My "pursuit of wisdom" is central to my programming paradigm today. Philosophy equipped me with a deep and critical understanding of the need for thorough logic. In applying this same mathematical logic to my programming career, I believe it's important to think before jumping in, to think more about logical flows than about the nuances of syntax, to balance aesthetics and efficacy.
